Franchising

Greenwich continues to open its doors to enterprising Filipinos who want to be part of the Philippines’ leading pizza brand!

  • You should have the financial capability to support and grow the business, possess entrepreneurial business
    capability in pursuing a long term business partnership with Greenwich, capability and the commitment to undergo the required training programs for business operations and possess good moral character with sound business repute in the community where
    the applicant resides and does business.


    You should be dynamic, success-driven entrepreneurs, and should possess the capability and willingness to learn our business and should enjoy working,
    communicating and relating with employees and customers.

  • Although past restaurant management may be helpful, Greenwich does not require previous franchise or restaurant experience. Greenwich will provide basic
    business management training that will enable you to run your franchise successfully.

  • Greenwich offers full store franchise units.

  • The total investment requirement ranges from 15 million to 31 million depending on the final store type & size. The size of the property required is dependent on
    the store model that will be developed in a particular market. Floor area requirement may range from 60 square meters to 150 square meters. There is also a
    franchise fee of 1.2 million (vat-in, net of 2% EWT).

  • The investment includes the initial set-up fee, design fees, construction cost, equipment and facilities, furniture and fixtures, air-conditioning system, signage, pre-opening expenses (training hours of crew and managers), initial inventory and initial hiring selection.

  • Based on franchisee and company data, the estimated return on investment is 4-5 years.

  • The royalty fee and advertising fee is five percent (5%) of gross sales. This fee will be used to provide the franchise store with support services (i.e., mass media advertising).

  • The franchise term is ten (10) years and is renewable, depending on the performance of the franchisee and the store.

  • The site should be in high foot traffic commercial/residential areas, accessible to both pedestrians and motorists.

  • Each location is different. Once a site is ready for construction, you can expect the store to be fully operational in 45-60 days.

  • Greenwich shall assist you in the architectural planning and construction process through its accredited architects and contractors. Should you wish to get non-Greenwich accredited suppliers, they will be requested to go through the accreditation process prior to building a store.

  • Greenwich will assist you in the recruitment and training of your store management team. You will be then become responsible for your team’s compensation and benefits.

  • The Greenwich Operations team will be your consultant and will continue to assist you and ensure alignment with company standards. They will also help you maximize sales and profitability.

  • Simply submit a letter of intent, vicinity map of proposed site, legal document certifying applicant’s ownership of the site (optional), and a detailed resume or biodata.
